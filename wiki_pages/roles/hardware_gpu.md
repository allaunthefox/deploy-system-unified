# hardware_gpu

**role**: `hardware/gpu`

**Unified GPU Stack (Integrated Battlemage)**
Automatic discovery and driver installation for AMD, Intel (including Arc/Battlemage), and NVIDIA GPUs across architectures (x86_64, aarch64, riscv64).
